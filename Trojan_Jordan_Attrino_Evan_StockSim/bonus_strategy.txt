Algorithm Description :
This strategy combines MA crossover filtering with tiered dip buying. A short (24-day)
and long (234-day) moving average detect market regime: a Golden Cross deploys all
accumulated cash immediately, while a Death Cross sells all shares and holds cash until
the next bull signal. While in the market, the algorithm checks daily how far price has
fallen from the 62-day rolling high — mild dips (>=2.75%) and moderate dips (>=~10.75%)
trigger scaled investments of the monthly budget, and severe crashes (>=18.75%) dump
all available cash regardless of MA regime. A rally filter (>=9.2% rise from the rolling
low) reduces buying to 40% of monthly capital to avoid chasing peaks.
